Through out my life I have seen a nice amount of cars, from the Pontiac GT to The Lamborghini Gallardo. I have fallen in love with cars that go fast and it has to be two doors. Also being red is a plus. I always figured I would want to spend my days speeding down the highway in a foreign vehicle, until I laid eyes on big red.

I have always been obsessed with two door vehicles but Big Red took it to a whole new level. She was sitting on the car lot all by her self surrounded by Mustangs , Chargers , and a few Camaros. It was funny because most of those cars were nice but she just stood out like a light in the darkness. I walked around and stared at her looking at her physique,admiring yet judging at the same time. I was looking for a reason to say no but couldn't find one.
